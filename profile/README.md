# Credit Plainly

Credit Plainly is a plain-English U.S. consumer credit education project focused on credit reports, credit scores, credit report disputes, and practical credit-building basics.

Website: https://www.creditplainly.com  
Contact: contact@creditplainly.com

## Start Here

- Credit Reports: https://www.creditplainly.com/credit-reports
- Credit Scores: https://www.creditplainly.com/credit-scores
- Credit Report Disputes: https://www.creditplainly.com/credit-report-disputes
- Resources: https://www.creditplainly.com/resources

## What We Publish

Credit Plainly focuses on educational guides, checklists, glossaries, and tools that help consumers understand credit information without quick-fix promises.

We do not promise score increases, item removals, approvals, or specific outcomes.
